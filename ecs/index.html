Frogger

Frogger is a fast-paced, endless arcade hopper where you guide an adventurous frog across a series of unpredictable hazards, rushing rivers, train tracks, and wild forests. The goal is simple: hop forward as far as you can without getting squished, swept away, or caught! Players use the w, a, s, and d keys to make the frog leap forward, left, right, or back, navigating through traffic jams and floating logs. You have a total of 3 lives. Use them wisely! Quick reflexes and smart timing are essential!
